Overview

This customised phonetic-based memory peg list is based on both the Harold Mangum System and the Major System.

Phonetic Sounds for 0 - 9

Numeral Phonetic Associated Consonants Typographic Major System Mnemonic
0 Suh, Zuh s, x, z, soft c z is the first letter of zero. The other letters have a similar sound.
1 Tuh, Duh t, d, th t & d have one downstroke and sound similar (some variant systems include “th”)
2 Nuh n n has two downstrokes and looks something like “2” on its side
3 Muh m m has three downstrokes and looks like a 3 on its side
4 Ruh r last letter of four, also 4 and R are almost mirror images of each other
5 Luh l l is the Roman Numeral for 50
6 Shuh, Chuh, Juh sh, soft ch, j, soft g, zh a script j has a lower loop / g is almost a 6 flipped over
7 Cuh, Guh k, hard c, hard g, hard ch, q, qu capital K “contains” two sevens
8 Fuh, Vuh f, v script f resembles a figure-8. V sounds similar
9 Puh, Buh p, b p is a mirror-image 9. b sounds similar and resembles a 9 rolled around
N/A N/A Vowel sounds, w,h,y These can be used anywhere without changing a word’s number value
